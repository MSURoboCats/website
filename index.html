WHAT WE'RE ABOUT

This team is a group of undergraduate engineering, and computer science students that work all year designing and developing an autonomous underwater vehicle (AUV) to compete in RoboNation's RoboSub competition. \ While this team is fairly small we are excited to show off the amazing creations of our team no matter how many things go wrong. Because of the small size of our team we all jump around to wherever is needed and have fun working on parts of the AUV we have no experience in. We are committed to having a good time learning through the process of creating (and breaking) our AUV and look forward to seeing how other teams approach this competition.
